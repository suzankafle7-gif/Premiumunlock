Frequently Asked Questions

What is OXEN Services?

OXEN Services is an educational resource hub that curates guides, tutorials, and access information for premium digital platforms including Crunchyroll, Grammarly, Pluralsight, Skillshare, Duolingo, Figma, Adobe Creative Cloud, and many others. We help students and professionals maximize free tiers, student discounts, and trial periods from trusted sources.

Are these services really free?

Most platforms we feature offer legitimate free tiers, ad-supported versions, or limited-time trials. For example, Duolingo is completely free, YouTube offers free content with ads, and Figma has a generous free starter plan. We always link to official websites and encourage users to respect terms of service. Never share accounts or use unauthorized methods.

How do I get student discounts?

Many platforms like Apple Music ($4.99/mo), Adobe Creative Cloud (60% off), The Economist, and Spotify offer verified student discounts. You typically need a valid .edu email address or verification through SheerID/UNiDAYS. Check each platform's pricing page for eligibility requirements and renewal policies.

Which AI tools are best for beginners?

We recommend starting with free tools like Google Gemini, Perplexity AI, and Quillbot. Jasper AI offers a 5-day trial for marketing copy, while Magnific AI provides free monthly credits for image upscaling. Always verify AI-generated information and never share sensitive personal data.

Can I stream sports for free?

Some platforms offer limited free content. FanCode has pay-per-view options for niche sports, SonyLIV streams select cricket matches for free, and YouTube hosts official highlights. For full access, DAZN, Prime Video, and Disney+ Hotstar require subscriptions but often run promotional offers.
