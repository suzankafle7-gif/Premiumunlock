Frequently Asked Questions

What is OXEN Services?

OXEN Services is an educational resource hub that curates guides, tutorials, and access information for premium digital platforms including Crunchyroll, Grammarly, Pluralsight, Skillshare, Duolingo, Figma, Adobe Creative Cloud, and many others. We help students and professionals maximize free tiers, student discounts, and trial periods from trusted sources.

Are these services really free?

Most platforms we feature offer legitimate free tiers, ad-supported versions, or limited-time trials. For example, Duolingo is completely free, YouTube offers free content with ads, and Figma has a generous free starter plan. We always link to official websites and encourage users to respect terms of service.

How do I get student discounts?

Many platforms like Apple Music, Adobe Creative Cloud, and The Economist offer verified student discounts. You typically need a valid .edu email address or verification through SheerID/UNiDAYS. Check each platform's pricing page for eligibility requirements.

Which AI tools are best for beginners?

We recommend starting with free tools like Google Gemini, Perplexity AI, and Quillbot. Jasper AI offers a trial for marketing copy, while Magnific AI provides free monthly credits for image upscaling. Always verify AI-generated information.

Can I suggest a new resource?

Absolutely! We welcome community suggestions for educational platforms. Visit our Contact page to submit recommendations with official links and brief descriptions.
